# Welcome to Beamable

Thank you for choosing Beamable to enhance your game's online capabilities. We’re excited to have you here! Whether you’re developing a fast-paced multiplayer shooter, a deep RPG with dynamic economies, or any other type of game, Beamable’s cloud-based services are designed to simplify your work—and make your game infinitely more enjoyable.

## What Makes Beamable Special

- **Built for Developers:** Get your project up and running in minutes with our SDKs for Unity and Unreal, comprehensive REST APIs, and intuitive command-line tools.

- **Complete LiveOps:** From identity management to sending emails, events, and analytics, Beamable bundles everything you need to keep your community engaged within a single portal.

- **Flexible and Extensible**: Explore the Live Services Marketplace or create custom microservices in C# and deploy them alongside Beamable’s core.

- **Scalable Infrastructure**: Run on fully managed public cloud, private cloud instances, or explore decentralized hosting networks—Beamable scales according to your ambitions.

## Core Services

- **Identity and Player Data:** Secure APIs for authentication (email, social media) and storage of profiles, progress, and statistics.
- **Game Economy:** Inventory management, in-game stores, and integrated purchases with configurable purchase flows.
- **LiveOps and Messaging**: Schedule events, send notifications, manage emails, and monitor player behavior from a single web dashboard.
- **Social and Community**: Built-in chat, friend lists, groups, and leaderboards to foster player interaction.
- **Multiplayer and Matchmaking**: Implement real-time or turn-based sessions, set custom matchmaking criteria, and use relay or peer-to-peer servers for smooth gameplay.
- **Analytics and BI**: Access metric dashboards, custom telemetry, and integration with third-party tools.

### Ready to Get Started?

- Register on the Beamable platform.
- Install the SDK in your game engine (Unity or Unreal).
- Create your first project using the CLI and explore the LiveOps portal.
- Join the community to ask questions and share tips.

We can’t wait to see what you’ll create. If you have any questions, we’re here to help every step of the way!
